## What MilePilot is

**MilePilot is the AI Business Operating System for the Self-Employed.**

Mileage tracking is our **foundation, not our destination**.

Every future feature must help self-employed people spend **less time on
administration** and **more time running their business**.

---

## Long-term pillars

1. **Capture Automatically**
   AutoPilot mileage tracking, background tracking, motion detection.

2. **Organise Automatically**
   Receipts, expenses, fuel, parking, VAT, business records.

3. **Understand Automatically**
   AI Bookkeeper, Business Health Score, Business Inbox, Business Briefings.

4. **Report Automatically**
   Professional PDFs, accountant packs, HMRC summaries, tax exports.

5. **Improve Automatically**
   AI insights, proactive reminders, business trends and recommendations.

---

## Product principles

- Simple before powerful.
- Automation before manual work.
- Trust before cleverness.
- Beautiful reports.
- Zero unnecessary admin.
- **One phase, one objective, one approval, one lock.**

---

## Architecture direction

- The **Business Hub** is the long-term centre of the product.
- The **AI Bookkeeper** is the intelligence layer.

Every decision should move MilePilot closer to becoming the **daily business
operating system for self-employed professionals**.

---

## What we do NOT do

- Do **not** add features simply because competitors have them.
- Build features that solve **real administrative pain**.

---

## Mission

**Remove administration from the lives of self-employed people.**
