Automatic tracking

For mileage recording without opening the app:

  • Location set to Always Allow (iPhone Settings → MilePilot → Location)
  • Start a shift before driving
  • Notifications allowed — trip auto-end and report alerts
  • Email saved below with reports turned on

Trips end automatically after 90 minutes without movement, then your report is emailed. Force-quitting the app or using Low Power Mode for long periods may delay this until you reopen MilePilot.
