Custom Date Range Report

Add up your business mileage between any two dates — then download or email one report for that exact period.

How it works

  1. Choose a start date and end date for the period you need.
  2. Check the preview for your total business miles between those dates.
  3. Tap Generate Report for a PDF, or Email this report.

Example “I need my business mileage from 23 May to 23 June — add up the total miles between these dates.” Pick those dates above, confirm the total in the preview, then generate your report.

Only reviewed business journeys are counted. Personal trips and journeys still awaiting review are not included.
