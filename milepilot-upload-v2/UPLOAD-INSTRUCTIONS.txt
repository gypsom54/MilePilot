UPLOAD STEPS (Cloudflare)
-------------------------
1. Unzip MilePilot-v8.22.2-CLOUDFLARE-UPLOAD.zip locally
2. Cloudflare → milepilot-app → Upload assets
3. Upload the FULL unzipped folder (index.html + js/ + all files)
4. Deploy — do NOT upload only version.txt
