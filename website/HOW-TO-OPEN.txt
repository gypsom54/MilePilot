QUICK OPEN
==========
1. Extract the zip (Extract All...)
2. Open the MilePilot-Website folder
3. Double-click DOUBLE-CLICK-ME.vbs

MANUAL OPEN (always works)
==========================
1. Open Microsoft Edge
2. Press Ctrl+O
3. Select index.html from the MilePilot-Website folder

See READ-ME-FIRST.txt for full help.
