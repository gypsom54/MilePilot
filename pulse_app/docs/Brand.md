---

## Positioning

Pulse is **not** a cluttered research tool or a dashboard of numbers.

It should feel like **Apple × WHOOP × Headspace** — premium software that disappears into the experience.

Users should think: *"This is different."*

---

## Visual personality

- **Deep graphite black** — the canvas
- **Electric cyan glow** — the heartbeat, the life
- **Soft white typography** — calm, readable, Apple-inspired
- **Generous whitespace** — breathing room everywhere
- **Glass and shadow** — depth without noise

## Logo

`PulseLogo` — heartbeat ECG line draws into the letter **P**, then **ULSE** fades in.

Tagline appears below: *Keep your finger on the Pulse.*

Never distort, recolour outside the design system, or animate with bounce/spin.

---

## Development principles

1. Optimise for **delight**, not speed.
2. Technology should **disappear**.
3. When unsure: choose the **calmer**, **simpler**, **more elegant** option.

---

## What Pulse is becoming

A personal research companion — organised, informed, confident.

Not inventory-first. Not calculator-first. **Experience-first.**
