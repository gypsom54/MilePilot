## Current focus

**Documentation + Brain integration** — making the codebase understandable and every screen Brain-driven.

---

## Principles (every sprint)

1. Do not add features outside the sprint goal
2. Perfect the experience before adding functionality
3. Update [Product Specs](./Product-Specs.md) and this roadmap
4. Update documentation in the same PR
5. No hardcoded copy in widgets

---

## North star

Users open Pulse and think:

> *"This is different."*

They feel **more organised**, **more informed**, and **more confident** — without trying.
