# Website Analyser

Reviews the business website for content gaps and structural improvements.

## Phase 1

Mock implementation only. No crawling or scraping.

## Input

- `businessId`
- `websiteUrl` (from memory)

## Output

`WebsiteAnalysisResult` with findings categorised by content gap, page quality, structure, and visibility.

## AuraCore connection

Findings feed the research pipeline and may generate Scout opportunities.
